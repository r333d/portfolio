Det andra rummet

An interactive art exhibition about a story and the city of Skellefteå, in northern Sweden.

concept · user experience · photography · graphic design

Visitors of the exhibition are invited to find special spots of the city of Skellefteå to unveil a story created by Helena Fagertun, a local writer.

To discover the places, each spot is hinted by four pictures of the location. Then the visitors can visit the place or navigate through a 3D model of Skellefteå that is installed in the exhibition. Finding a spot grants access to a chapter of the story in the website.

The exhibition was created for the annual event that Skellefteå hosts about storytelling in April. We created it to be aligned with other events created by the museum Anna Nordlander about the city and how it seen with the perspective of the children, so the story and places are selected to be special for the young.

The project was part of the master in interaction design and I worked in partnership with my classmate Tommy Nilsson. The exhibition and the rest of the events were a success, attracting a new type of audience to visit the museum and being showcased in the most important local media.
