Emblemas

A project of documentation and infographic illustration presented in a book.

Illustration · data research · book design

Gestural emblems, like spoken languages, are not universal. We change the gesture or their meaning depending on the area and, specially, the cultural roots.

Showing the middle and index fingers pointing up, slightly apart from each other and with the palm inwards, in many places means two, victory or happiness. In contrast, in the United Kingdom and other Anglo-Saxon countries such Australia and New Zealand this is an offensive gesture because of its historical origin. It symbolizes challenge, contempt and ridicule. Differences like these have led to misunderstandings and, even, unfortunate advertising campaigns.

The book shows the differences between emblems that are used in different places. It is full of infographic illustrations that explain every gesture.
