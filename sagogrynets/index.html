Sagogrynets Resa

I game created during my time as UX designer intern at North Kingdom.

iOS Android

UX · UI · Game Design

Sagogrynets Resa continues a beautiful Christmas tradition of the Sweden's most powerful media channel SVT.

My work was to generate ideas for level design, controls and UI design, in addition to user flows, wireframes and conduct QA testing for the supported devices.

The objective of the game is to light the stars while building your own dream rocket.

The game quickly climbed charts for top spots on Google Play and AppStore in Sweden, Denmark, Norway and Finland.

I'm always looking for interesting challenges and new friends.
